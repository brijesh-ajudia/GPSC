Momentum Rule

Work ke beech 2–3 hrs study block break mat karo – momentum tootega. Ek block complete karo, phir shift karo.

🚶

50-Min Rule

Har 50 min study ke baad 5–10 min walk. Brain rest + blood flow dono improve hoga.

🛑

Weekly Off

Weekly 1 din full off (family/fun) – burnout nahi hoga. Sunday light revision only, no new topic.

📊

Monthly Self-Review

Score track karo Excel mein. Target GS1 120+. Dekho kya improve hua, kahan time waste hua.

🥗

Diet & Health

Gujarati homemade food + 4 litre paani + 30 min walk daily. Healthy body = sharp mind.

📖

3x Revision Rule

Har topic 3 baar padho: 1st reading → 2nd notes → 3rd only notes + MCQ. Never skip revision.

🎓

No Coaching Needed

Coaching mat lo abhi. YouTube + books + self-study enough hai first attempt ke liye. Save money.

📋 Self-Practice Method (Step-by-Step)

✍️

Daily Routine (30–60 min)

Syllabus se 2–3 questions choose karo (PYQ Mains papers) · 7–15 min/question · A4 sheet pe likho · Word limit: 150–250 words

🏗️

Answer Structure

Intro (2–3 lines) → Body (Facts + Data + Gujarat angle + Examples) → Multi-dimensional → Conclusion (way-forward)

🔍

Self-Evaluation

Content: Points miss? · Structure: Clear headings? · Language: Simple, no errors · Keywords: Directive words used correctly?

📊

Weekly / Monthly

1 full essay (1200–1500 words) weekly · Sunday: 4–5 answers + peer review (Reddit/GPSC forums) · Month 11: Full Mains mocks
